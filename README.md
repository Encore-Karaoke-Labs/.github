# 👋 Hiya! We're Encore Karaoke Labs.
We are a multinational team of developers, artists and musicians who have a collective love for karaoke, from the Philippines, to the United Kingdom. We believe in making everyone **feel like a star**.

We are the makers of [Encore Karaoke](https://github.com/Encore-Karaoke-Labs/Encore-Karaoke), software that turns your computer into a full-blown karaoke machine, and [Encore MIDI Studio](https://github.com/Encore-Karaoke-Labs/Encore-Studio), the web-based MIDI editor with support for lyrics.
